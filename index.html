What We Do

Security Basics, Mastered.

We don't sell "magic boxes." We do the hard work of configuration, auditing, and helping you choose the right vendors.

Most Popular

External Attack Surface

We map and monitor your internet-facing assets to identify what attackers see before they strike. This is a continuous discovery process.

What's Included:
  • Shadow IT Discovery: Identifying unknown subdomains, forgotten servers, and dev environments.
  • Vulnerability Mapping: CVE checking on all exposed assets.
  • Open Port Analysis: Detection of exposed RDP, SSH, or database ports.

Identity (IDM) Security Audit

Identity is the new perimeter. We perform a deep-dive audit of your Microsoft Entra (Azure AD) and identity provider configurations.

What's Included:
  • Conditional Access Review: Ensuring MFA is enforced and policies are gap-free.
  • Privileged Identity (PIM): Auditing Global Admins and Just-In-Time access.
  • App Registration Audit: Identifying risky OAuth applications.

Microsoft 365 Tenant Security

Your M365 tenant holds your most critical data. We review the ecosystem beyond just the login screen to prevent data leakage and fraud.

What's Included:
  • Exchange Online: Anti-phishing policies, SPF/DKIM/DMARC, and forwarding rules.
  • SharePoint & Teams: External sharing settings and guest access review.
  • Audit Logging: Verifying unified audit logs are active and capturing data.

Edge Configuration Audit

Your firewall is only as good as its ruleset. We review the configurations of your perimeter devices (Firewalls, VPN Concentrators).

What's Included:
  • Ruleset Analysis: Identifying "Any/Any" rules, unused objects, and shadowed rules.
  • VPN Hardening: Checking encryption standards and split-tunneling policies.
  • Management Plane: Verifying administrative access is restricted.

Strategic Advisory & Selection

Beyond audits, we act as your impartial advisor. We help you cut through vendor marketing noise and assess supply chain risk.

What's Included:
  • Tool Evaluation: Selection guidance for EDR, SIEM, and SOC services.
  • Vendor Risk (TPRM): Auditing your MSP or supply chain for security gaps.
  • Roadmaps: Phased, budget-aware security improvement plans.

Cyber Hygiene & Readiness

Getting the basics right prevents 90% of attacks. We verify the foundational controls that actually stop ransomware.

What's Included:
  • Endpoint Protection: Verifying EDR coverage and policy settings.
  • Backup Verification: Ensuring backups are immutable and air-gapped.
  • Local Admin Review: Checking for shared local administrator passwords (LAPS).
